[1] During 1997 Angelina Jolie appeared in the TV movie "George Wallace". [2] In 1998 she appeared in the TV movie "Gia". [3] After filming "Gia" Angelina attended writing and directing classes at New York University. [4] In 1999 she appeared in "Pushing Tin", "Playing by Heart", "Has Anybody Seen my Baby", "The Bone Collector", and "Girl Interrupted". [5] In 2000 she appeared in "Dancing in the Dark", "Gone in 60 Seconds", and "Beyond Borders". [6] She appeared in the movie "Tomb Raider" which was shot in 2000 and due for release in 2001. [7] At 25 Angelina had earned three Golden Globe Awards. [8] One award was for her performance in the TNT miniseries "George Wallace" and one was for her role as best actress in a miniseries or TV movie in the HBO film "GIA". [9] She received both a Golden Globe Award and an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress for "Girl Interrupted" in 1999. [10] Angelina was married briefly to actor Johnny Lee Miller and the marriage ended in divorce. [11] She married Billy Bob Thornton in May 2000.
