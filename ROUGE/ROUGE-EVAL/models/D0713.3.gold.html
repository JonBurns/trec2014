[1] Pakistan has refused to sign the Nuclear Non-Proliferation Treaty (NPT), citing its need to maintain a credible nuclear deterrence against aggression from India. [2] It has asserted it would take appropriate steps to safeguard its national security, while maintaining a policy of restraint regarding its nuclear capabilities. [3] Following India's nuclear tests on May 11 and 13, 1998, Pakistan felt compelled to respond with six of its own -- five on May 28 and one on May 30.
